Bonnie "Prince" Billy (real name Joseph Will Oldham) is an American singer-songwriter, known for his "do-it-yourself punk aesthetic and blunt honesty".


His music has been likened to Americana, country, indie rock, punk, and folk.


Oldham adopted the 'Bonnie "Prince" Billy' moniker in 1998, drawing inspiration from many different sources:

"The name has so many different references that it could almost have a life of its own. Bonnie Prince Charlie has such a beautiful ring to it, and I was very conscious of appropriating that mellifluous sound.

And I was also thinking about the name Nat King Cole. But it wasn't until later, and this may have been subconscious, that I remembered that Billy the Kid was William Bonney or Billy Bonney".
